# Stitch Prompt — Build the WithAgents Brand System and Website

You are designing and building out the full brand system and website direction for **WithAgents**.

This is not a generic AI startup brand. It is an umbrella brand for:
- products
- open-source projects
- essays, field notes, and blog posts
- practical applied work helping organizations introduce agentic systems into real workflows

The commercial layer should be **subtle**. The public impression should be:
- here are the products
- here is what we are learning
- here is what we are building in the open
- here is the thinking behind the systems

The site should feel like a serious product-and-ideas home first, with the more applied work implied and discoverable, but not loudly sold.

## Primary Brand Thesis

**WithAgents** should feel:
- minimal
- crisp
- black-first
- premium
- technical but restrained
- thoughtful rather than trendy
- modern without looking like a neon cyberpunk parody
- credible to product teams, engineering leaders, and enterprises
- interesting and distinctive enough to feel like a real design-conscious brand

The tone should sit somewhere between:
- a product studio
- a systems publication
- a sophisticated open-source lab
- a quiet applied research and implementation brand

Avoid anything that feels like:
- a loud AI agency
- a gimmicky hackathon brand
- a generic gradient SaaS landing page
- a flashy consumer crypto aesthetic
- an overdone “future” cliché

## Visual Direction

Base the work on a **Hyper-black** design language:
- flat black background foundation
- very dark charcoal and near-black panels
- crisp edges
- clean spacing
- subtle glow only when justified
- premium restraint
- clean typography
- sharp component rhythm
- modern, high-end product taste

## Content Direction

Use real-feeling content in the style below. Expand it, improve it, and make it feel like a polished brand.

### Core Positioning Copy
**WithAgents** is a home for applied agent design: products, open-source work, and writing about what holds up in production.

### Products

- **Runbooks** — opinionated workflows for triage, analysis, and execution across real internal systems
- **Memory Layer** — practical patterns for preserving context across sessions, tools, and multi-step work

- Avoid excessive gradients

- Favor typography, layout, spacing, and precise accent use over decoration
