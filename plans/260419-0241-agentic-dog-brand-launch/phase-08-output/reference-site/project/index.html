§02  /  The process

A day at the studio.

Four beats. No dashboards, no plumbing.
Beat 01 — Morning

You arrive with a problem.

Drop a doc, a link, a half-finished thought. The studio reads it before you finish your coffee.

┌─── 08:42 ──────┐ │ new brief │ │ ↳ marlowe │ │ ↳ mira │ └────────────────┘
Beat 02 — Mid-morning

Agents confer in the margins.

Hestia routes, Marlowe reads, Pascal runs numbers. You watch the thread like a chat, not a graph.

hestia ▸ routed (3) marlowe ▸ reading… pascal ▸ 14 queries verity ▸ standing by
Beat 03 — Afternoon

You steer, not babysit.

Tap an agent on the shoulder. Rewrite a paragraph. Reject a draft. They learn on the spot.

◆ draft v1 → rejected ◆ draft v2 → noted ◆ draft v3 → accepted (in your voice, finally)
Beat 04 — Close

Work ships with your name.

Signed, reviewed, accountable. Every artefact carries a chain of custody you can read.

shipped: memo-q2.md by: you w/: marlowe, juno, verity at: 16:31 ✓ reviewed
