About WithAgents.

A home for applied agent design.

The Thesis

We build environments that prioritize systems over spectacle. The "Technical Curator" philosophy demands that every interaction is a deliberate act of design, rejecting the vague "chat" paradigms in favor of structured, purposeful workflows.

Our approach to applied agent design centers on creating high-fidelity instruments. We believe intelligence is most valuable when integrated seamlessly into complex, domain-specific tasks, demanding interfaces that respect the user's expertise and time.
