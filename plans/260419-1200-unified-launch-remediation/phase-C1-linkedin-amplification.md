**Owner:** Nick (posting) + Sonnet helper (asset prep, scheduler)
**Effort:** ~80h over 45–60 days

## Context

LinkedIn is the primary B2B surface for the consulting practice. Algorithm rewards native content, long dwell time, and author-on-author engagement. Article format boosts reach for longform but has a hard algorithmic cap: posting >4 Articles/week suppresses feed distribution of subsequent items for 7d. Source: R2 §1.

**Locked constraints (parent plan.md #44–54):**
- Article cap: 3–4/wk → **16–18 Articles** across 45–60d (revised from 18–20 after red-team math reconciliation, see C1.2)
- Remaining days: feed post only (excerpt + CTA to withagents.dev)
- Hashtag strategy: ≤3/post, primary `#AgenticDevelopment`
- Daily feed-post all 45 days regardless of Article status
- **Weekday preference relaxed from Tue/Wed only → Mon–Thu** (flagship Days 7 and 14 from Tue launch date fall on Mon — unsolvable with strict Tue/Wed rule)

Source assets: `posts/post-NN-*/day-NN.linkedin.md` (generated by daily-push pipeline, Track B).

### C1.2 — Article paste-day selection matrix (16–18 days)

**Fix:** Not every day warrants an Article. Flagship days get Article format; filler days get feed-only. Selection matrix:

| Criterion | Threshold | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Day is a flagship (Day 1, 7, 14, 22, 30, 45) | Yes | Article mandatory (highest priority) |
| `day-NN.linkedin.md` word count ≥ 900 | Yes | Article candidate |
| Day contains unique chart/diagram SVG | Yes | Article candidate |
| Week already has 4 Articles published | Yes | Apply conflict-resolution rule below |
| Day is Sat or Sun | Yes | Feed-only (lower B2B reach) |

**Conflict-resolution rule (flagship × 4/wk cap):** when a flagship day would be the 5th Article in its ISO week, DO NOT demote the flagship to feed-only. Instead, ADVANCE one non-flagship Article earlier in the same week to an adjacent week that has <4 Articles (Mon-Thu weekday days only). If no adjacent slot available, the non-flagship Article demotes to feed-only; the flagship keeps Article format. Flagships are load-bearing; non-flagships are fungible.

**Actual schedule (Tue 2026-04-21 launch → 45-day calendar, Mon–Thu Article preference):**

| Week | Days in week | Article candidates (flagship + high-priority) | Articles scheduled | Feed-only days |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| W1 | Days 1–6 (Tue-Sun) | D1 (flagship), D2, D3, D4 | 4 (D1 Tue / D2 Wed / D3 Thu / D4 Mon→no, use D2 Wed skip, use D3 Thu, D1 Tue) | D5 Sat, D6 Sun, off |
| W2 | Days 7–13 (Mon-Sun) | D7 (flagship Mon), D8 Tue, D9 Wed, D10 Thu | 4 | D11 Fri, D12 Sat, D13 Sun |
| W3 | Days 14–20 (Mon-Sun) | D14 (flagship Mon), D15 Tue, D16 Wed, D17 Thu | 4 | D18 Fri, D19 Sat, D20 Sun |
| W4 | Days 21–27 (Mon-Sun) | D22 (flagship Tue), D23 Wed, D24 Thu + 1 bonus | 4 | D21 Mon, D25 Fri, D26 Sat, D27 Sun |
| W5 | Days 28–34 (Mon-Sun) | D29 Tue, D30 (flagship Wed), D31 Thu | 3 (Day 32/33 off-days absorb; Mon D28 feed) | D28, D31 post D32/33 off, D34 |
| W6 | Days 35–41 (Mon-Sun) | D35 Mon, D36 Tue, D37 Wed, D38 Thu | 3 (spread) | others feed |
| W7 | Days 42–45 (Mon-Thu) | D45 (flagship Thu) + 1 (Tue or Wed) | 2 | D42 Mon, D43, D44 feed |
